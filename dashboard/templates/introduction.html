Spring 2016 Fare Survey Overview

The Spring 2016 Fare survey lasted from February 20, 2016 to May 1, 2016. The project was conducted at a system-wide level and used hand-held tablets to collect data. The project was completed one month ahead of schedule and achieved a 30% reduction in cost compared to the paper-based 2012 Fare survey.


The main objectives for the survey include collecting data for studying the relationship between fare change and ridership change, performing service planning and analysis, completing equity analysis and developing a Title VI report. Survey questionnaires gather the following information:


  • Fare type and payment method, fare usage, route, transfers
  • College student (yes or no)
  • Race, gender, age (for cross-referencing fare type), and income level
  • Transit dependency
  • English proficiency and other languages spoken at home
